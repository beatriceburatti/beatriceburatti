About the project

Working at Giacimenti Urbani means being part of a non-profit organisation that promotes environmental sustainability and circular economy through cultural initiatives and community engagement. Since May 2026 I have been contributing to its digital transformation, bringing my perspective as a Gen Z user to reshape how the association communicates online, with a focus on reaching new and younger audiences.

Activities & skills

My main activities focus on managing the association's digital presence across Facebook and Instagram, defining comprehensive communication strategies, and producing content and copy to support the non-profit organization's digital transformation. I am also responsible for curating and updating the official WordPress website by publishing articles, news, and event updates, while actively assisting in public relations and on-site event coordination.

My approach has been hands-on from the start: I maintained a consistent daily presence, transitioned our content from a square format to 4:5 vertical to better match how people scroll, and introduced Reels featuring members who speak directly about our initiatives. Highlighting real people, rather than just graphics, makes our content feel spontaneous and authentic, which I believe is the most effective way to build trust. To reach a wider audience and raise awareness around sustainability and community events, I also started producing carousels specifically designed to engage those who are not yet familiar with Giacimenti Urbani.
