Hi, my name is Beatrice Buratti and I'm a multidisciplinary designer based in Milan, focused on graphic design and communication. Driven by curiosity and inspired by art, culture, and everyday life.

About me:

Born in 2001 in Rome, I studied at Politecnico di Milano, earning a Bachelor's Degree in Industrial Product Design in 2023. I later completed my Master's Degree in Interior and Spatial Design in March 2026, enriched by a semester abroad at Die Angewandte in Vienna.

My background is multidisciplinary, with experience across graphic design and communication. I have worked in a design studio and a non-profit organisation, where I managed editorial plans, social media, printed materials, and events. Working across these varied environments has trained me to meet tight deadlines, adapt quickly, and maintain visual quality across different formats and audiences.

Outside work, you'll find me visiting exhibitions, taking photos, spotting dogs on the streets (99% accuracy on race detector), tasting new foods and discovering new spots, or getting lost in a museum.
